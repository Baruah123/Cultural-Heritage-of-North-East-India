The Art & Music of Assam

Explore the rich cultural heritage of Assam through its traditional art forms and soul-soothing music.

Sattriya Dance

Sattriya is one of the classical dance forms of India, originating from Assam. It was introduced by the saint Srimanta Sankardeva and is still performed in various cultural events across the state.

Bihu Dance

The Bihu Dance is an energetic and vibrant folk dance of Assam, traditionally performed during the Bihu festival. It is accompanied by musical instruments like the dhol and pepa, bringing joy and unity.

Pottery Art

Pottery is a centuries-old craft in Assam. Skilled artisans create exquisite pieces of earthenware that reflect the unique cultural identity of the region.

Assamese Folk Music

Assamese folk music includes various traditional songs, such as Bihu songs, Husori, and Tokari Geet. The melodious tunes of the flute and dhol bring life to these folk traditions.
