Cultural Delicacies of Assam

Discover the authentic flavors of Assam, a blend of tradition, nature, and simplicity.

Assamese Thali

The Assamese Thali is a combination of rice, lentils, mustard greens, and fish, often served with traditional pickles and sides. It is known for its balanced flavors and simplicity.

Bamboo Shoot Curry

Bamboo shoot curry is a staple of Assamese cuisine, offering a unique tangy flavor. The shoots are prepared with spices, herbs, and often combined with meat or fish.

Pitha

Pitha is a traditional Assamese sweet or savory dish made from rice flour, coconut, and sesame seeds. It's popular during festivals and special occasions.

Rice Beer (Apong)

Apong is a traditional rice beer brewed by the tribal communities of Assam. It is an integral part of many local festivals and celebrations.
