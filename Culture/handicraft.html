Assam's Traditional Handicrafts

Explore the unique crafts from the land of Assam, enriched with cultural heritage and skillful artisanship.

Bell Metal Craft

Bell Metal Craft is a famous traditional craft in Assam, primarily produced in Sarthebari. The artisans create items like utensils, decorative pieces, and religious artifacts.

Bamboo Craft

Bamboo and cane crafts are widely popular in Assam, where artisans create baskets, mats, furniture, and other household items from bamboo, reflecting Assam's eco-friendly lifestyle.

Wood Carving

Assamese artisans have mastered the art of wood carving, producing exquisite furniture, sculptures, and religious idols, often depicting intricate patterns from local folklore.

Terracotta Pottery

Assam's terracotta pottery stands out for its earthy tones and unique designs. Artisans create both utilitarian and decorative items that have cultural and religious importance.
